Your Questions, Answered

We know that investing in online advertising can bring up a few questions. Here are some honest and simple answers to help you feel confident.

Q. What’s a realistic starting budget for a small business in Vancouver?

A. You are in complete control of the budget. For a business that’s serious about growth in Vancouver, a good starting point is typically between $1,000 to $2,000 per month in ad spend. Our main job is to make every single dollar of that budget work as hard as possible for you.

Q. Where does my money actually go?

It’s simple! Your money is split into two parts. The “ad spend” goes directly to Google to pay for the clicks on your ads. Our “management fee” is for our team of experts who work every day to make sure that ad spend turns into real customers.

Q. Am I locked into a long-term contract?

Not at all. We don’t believe in scary, long-term contracts. We start with a 3-month plan to give us time to get you great results. After that, it’s a simple month-to-month service. We want to earn your business every month.

Q. I tried Google Ads myself and it didn’t work. Why is this different?

That’s a very common story. The difference is that this is all we do! We use our experience and smart technology to avoid the common mistakes that waste money. We focus on building campaigns that are designed to find customers, not just get clicks.

Q. What kind of ‘results’ should I actually expect?

We focus on the results that actually grow your business: more phone calls, more quote requests, and more sales. We don’t get distracted by flashy numbers that don’t help your bottom line.

Q. Can I really compete with businesses that have huge budgets?

Yes! The great thing about Google Ads is that it’s not always about who spends the most. Google rewards ads that are the most helpful and relevant to the person searching. We can often win by being smarter, not just by spending more.

Q. Do I need a special website for these ads to work?

Sending visitors to a focused page works much better than sending them to a general homepage. This is called a “landing page.” If you don’t have one, don’t worry at all—we’ll guide you and help you get exactly what you need.

Q. What’s the real difference between SEO and Google Ads?

Think of it this way: SEO is like buying a house—it’s a fantastic long-term investment. Google Ads is like renting a perfect storefront on a busy street—you get business and foot traffic immediately. They work best when you do both!

Q. Who owns the Google Ads account?

You do, always. It’s your account and your data. We believe in total transparency, so you will always have full ownership and access to everything.

Q. Why should I choose your agency over a cheaper freelancer?

With us, you get a whole team of certified experts, access to powerful advertising software, and the peace of mind that comes with a Google Premier Partner. We provide a level of support and expertise that’s hard for a single person to match.
